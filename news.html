Paris men's week opens with folding chairs and concrete.

Prada opened with a procession of washed linens and rubber soles — Miuccia's quiet rebellion against the season's maximalism. Guests sat on folding chairs in a raw concrete hall. No front row hierarchy.

Paris · MFW

16mm athlete footage, no synthetic crowd noise.

Nike shares climbed 6% after the brand announced a return to performance-first storytelling. The new campaign drops athlete footage shot on 16mm — no AI upscaling, no synthetic crowd noise. Analysts call it a pivot away from lifestyle collabs.

Nike · NYSE

1988 Tribe tee restocked three times in 48 hours.

Stussy's spring archive sale moved $2.1M in 48 hours — mostly international buyers. The 1988 International Stüssy Tribe tee restocked three times. Resellers are already listing at 4× markup on Grailed.

Stussy · Archive

Afterparty in a disused Milan tram depot.

Gucci's Sabato De Sarno showed a collection built entirely from deadstock jacquard. The afterparty was held in a disused tram depot. Anna Wintour left early — reportedly to catch a flight to Copenhagen for Ganni's pre-collection preview.

Milan · MFW

Box logo stitched inside the tongue — invisible until worn.

Supreme × MM6 Maison Margiela drops Thursday. The leak shows deconstructed tabi boots with Supreme box logo stitching inside the tongue — invisible until worn. Stock expected to sell out in under 90 seconds.

Supreme · MM6

Japan and Korea offset China softness in Q2 earnings.

LVMH reported soft luxury demand in China but strong growth in Japan and Korea. Bernard Arnault cited 'younger Asian consumers trading up from streetwear into maison labels' as the quarter's surprise driver.

LVMH · Earnings

94% traceable. Styled with vintage Danish military surplus.

Ganni's pre-collection featured biodegradable sequins made from algae cellulose. Every look was styled with vintage Danish military surplus. The brand claims 94% of the collection is traceable to source.

Copenhagen · CFW

CdG takes full ground floor. Pop-up on Lexington next month.

DSM London is closing its basement level for a six-month renovation. Comme des Garçons will occupy the entire ground floor during the rebuild. A temporary pop-up opens on Lexington Street next month.

Dover Street Market · London

Pre-1995 Americana only. Queue stretched two blocks.

BEAMS opened a concept store dedicated to archive Americana — only items produced before 1995. The launch queue stretched two blocks. Inside: deadstock Levi's, vintage Patagonia, and a listening booth playing Japanese city pop on cassette.

Tokyo · Shibuya

Unchanged colorway since 1982. StockX prices climbing.

New Balance 990v7 restock sold out globally in 11 minutes. The grey colorway — unchanged since 1982 — remains the brand's most requested silhouette. Resale prices already climbing on StockX.

New Balance · Boston

Nigerian label draws orders from Seoul and Tokyo buyers.

Pitti Uomo 106 drew record attendance from Asian buyers. The standout moment: a Nigerian label showing hand-woven agbada reinterpreted as oversized tailoring. Buyers from Seoul and Tokyo placed orders on the spot.

Florence · Pitti Uomo

Tri-ferg on inside collar only. Kits reissued from deadstock.

Palace's summer drop features a collaboration with Umbro — football kits reissued in deadstock polyester from the 1994 World Cup. The tri-ferg logo appears on the inside collar only.
